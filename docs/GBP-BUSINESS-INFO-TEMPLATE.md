## Questions & Answers (Pre-populate)

### Q1: Do you offer consultations in Arabic?
**A**: Yes, we provide full bilingual support in both English and Arabic. All our consultation reports, infographics, and educational materials are available in your preferred language.

### Q2: How long does it take to receive my consultation report?
**A**: Our AI analysis is completed within 24 hours. After that, a certified medical specialist reviews the analysis, and you'll receive your comprehensive consultation report within 48-72 hours.

### Q3: Is the first consultation really free?
**A**: Yes! Your first comprehensive medical analysis and consultation is completely free. This includes AI analysis, specialist review, detailed report, and visual infographics.

### Q4: Do you accept insurance?
**A**: We provide detailed consultation reports and receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider for potential reimbursement. Please check with your insurance company about their telemedicine coverage policies.

### Q5: What documents do I need to submit?
**A**: You can submit any medical documents you have, including: medical reports, lab test results, X-rays, MRI/CT scans, doctor's notes, prescription history, and a description of your symptoms and medical history.

### Q6: Is my medical information secure?
**A**: Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and comply with international medical data privacy standards. Your information is confidential and never shared without your explicit consent.

### Q7: Can I get a second opinion on my diagnosis?
**A**: Yes, our service is perfect for second opinions. Our AI system and medical specialists will review your existing diagnosis and provide an independent analysis and recommendations.

### Q8: Do you provide emergency medical services?
**A**: No, we provide non-emergency medical consultation and analysis services. If you have a medical emergency, please call 997 (Saudi Arabia emergency number) or visit the nearest emergency room immediately.
