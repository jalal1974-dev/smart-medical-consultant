# Google Business Profile Optimization & Content Calendar

## Overview

This guide provides strategies, best practices, and a content calendar for optimizing your Google Business Profile to maximize local search visibility and customer engagement.

---

## Part 1: Post Optimization Strategy

### Post Types and When to Use Them

| Post Type | Purpose | Best For | Frequency |
|-----------|---------|----------|-----------|
| **What's New** | Announce updates, features, news | New services, platform updates | 1-2x/week |
| **Event** | Promote events, webinars, awareness days | Health awareness campaigns | As needed |
| **Offer** | Share promotions and special deals | Free consultations, discounts | 1x/week |
| **Product** | Highlight specific services | Service packages, new offerings | 1x/month |

### Post Best Practices

**Length**:
- Minimum: 100 characters
- Maximum: 1,500 characters
- Optimal: 150-300 characters (short and engaging)

**Format**:
- Start with an emoji or attention-grabber
- Use bullet points for readability
- Include a clear call-to-action (CTA)
- Add relevant hashtags (3-5 maximum)

**Media**:
- Always include a photo or video
- Image size: 750x750px (square) or 1200x900px (landscape)
- Video: 30 seconds maximum
- High-quality, relevant visuals

**CTA Buttons**:
- Book
- Order online
- Buy
- Learn more
- Sign up
- Call now

### Photo Categories and Quantities

### Photo Ideas for Telemedicine Service

**Interior/Office Photos**:
- Reception area
- Waiting room
- Consultation rooms
- Medical equipment
- Computer workstations
- Conference rooms
- Break room (humanizes your team)

**Team Photos**:
- Individual headshots of specialists
- Group photo of entire team
- Specialists reviewing consultations
- Team meetings
- Training sessions
- Casual team moments

**At Work Photos**:
- Specialist reviewing medical reports
- AI system in action (screen showing analysis)
- Video consultation setup
- Team collaboration
- Quality assurance processes
- Research and development

**Service/Product Photos**:
- Sample consultation reports (anonymized)
- Infographic examples (no patient data)
- Educational materials
- Platform interface screenshots
- Mobile app interface
- Patient dashboard view

**Technology Photos**:
- Advanced medical software
- AI analysis systems
- Secure data servers
- Telemedicine equipment
- High-tech medical devices

### Photo Optimization Tips

**Technical**:
- Resolution: Minimum 720x720px
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 10KB - 5MB
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1) or landscape (4:3, 16:9)

**Content**:
- Well-lit (natural light preferred)
- In focus and sharp
- No watermarks or logos
- No promotional text overlays
- Professional and clean
- Authentic (not stock photos)

**Metadata**:
- Add descriptive file names (e.g., "medical-specialist-consultation.jpg")
- Include location data if applicable
- Add alt text descriptions

## Part 3: Review Management Strategy

### Encouraging Reviews

**Timing**:
- Ask after successful consultation completion
- Wait 24-48 hours after service delivery
- Don't ask during negative interactions

**Methods**:

1. **Direct Request (In-Person/Call)**:
```
"We're glad we could help with your medical consultation. If you found our service valuable, we'd appreciate if you could share your experience on Google. It helps other patients find us."
```

2. **Email Template**:
```
Subject: How was your consultation with Smart Medical Consultant?

Dear [Patient Name],

Thank you for choosing Smart Medical Consultant for your medical analysis. We hope our AI-powered consultation and specialist review provided valuable insights.

If you were satisfied with our service, we'd be grateful if you could take a moment to share your experience on Google. Your feedback helps us improve and helps other patients make informed decisions.

[Leave a Review Button]

Thank you for your trust in our service!

Best regards,
Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

3. **SMS Template**:
```
Hi [Name], thank you for using Smart Medical Consultant! If you found our service helpful, please consider leaving us a review: [short link]. Your feedback means a lot! - SMC Team
```

4. **Website Integration**:
- Add "Leave a Review" button in patient dashboard
- Show after consultation completion
- Include in follow-up emails

### Review Response Framework

**Response Time**: Within 24 hours (maximum 48 hours)

**Response Length**: 50-200 words

**Response Structure**:
1. Thank the reviewer
2. Address specific points mentioned
3. Offer resolution (if negative)
4. Invite further communication (if needed)
5. Sign with team name

### Response Templates by Rating

**5-Star Review**:
```
Thank you so much for your wonderful review, [Name]! 🌟 We're thrilled to hear that our AI-powered analysis and specialist review helped you [specific benefit mentioned]. Providing clear, comprehensive medical consultations in both English and Arabic is our passion. If you have any future health questions, we're here 24/7. Wishing you continued good health! - Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

**4-Star Review**:
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Name]! ⭐ We're glad our [specific service] was helpful for you. We noticed you mentioned [area for improvement], and we're always working to enhance our service. If there's anything specific we can do better, please don't hesitate to reach out at [contact]. Your health and satisfaction are our priorities! - Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

**3-Star Review**:
```
Thank you for sharing your experience, [Name]. We appreciate your honest feedback about [specific issue]. We'd love to learn more about how we can improve your experience. Please contact us directly at [phone/email] so we can address your concerns personally. Your input helps us serve our patients better. - Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

**2-Star Review**:
```
We're sorry to hear about your experience, [Name]. This isn't the level of service we strive to provide. We take your feedback about [specific issue] very seriously. Please reach out to us at [phone/email] so we can understand what went wrong and make it right. We're committed to your satisfaction and health. - Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

**1-Star Review**:
```
We sincerely apologize for your disappointing experience, [Name]. This is not acceptable, and we want to make things right. Please contact us immediately at [phone/email] or [manager's direct contact] so we can address this urgently. Your health and trust are our top priorities, and we're committed to resolving this. - Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

### Handling Specific Review Scenarios

**Fake/Spam Review**:
- Don't respond publicly
- Report to Google as inappropriate
- Flag for removal if violates policies

**Competitor Review**:
- Respond professionally
- Don't accuse or argue
- Focus on your service quality
- Report if clearly fake

**Review with Medical Privacy Concerns**:
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We take patient privacy very seriously. To discuss your specific case, please contact us directly at [private contact] so we can address your concerns confidentially. - Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

**Demanding Review (Asking for Refund/Compensation)**:
```
We're sorry you weren't satisfied with our service. We'd like to resolve this privately. Please contact us at [phone/email] so we can discuss this matter directly and find a solution. - Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

## Part 4: Q&A Optimization

### Proactive Q&A Strategy

**Why Pre-populate Q&A**:
- Control the narrative
- Address common concerns
- Improve SEO (keywords in Q&A)
- Save time answering repetitive questions

### Essential Questions to Add

**1. Service-Related**:
- What services do you offer?
- Do you provide telemedicine consultations?
- Can I get a second medical opinion?
- What medical documents do you accept?

**2. Language & Accessibility**:
- Do you offer consultations in Arabic?
- Is your platform available 24/7?
- Do you have wheelchair access? (if applicable)

**3. Pricing & Insurance**:
- How much does a consultation cost?
- Is the first consultation really free?
- Do you accept insurance?
- What payment methods do you accept?

**4. Process & Timeline**:
- How long does it take to get results?
- What is your consultation process?
- How do I submit my medical reports?
- When will I receive my consultation report?

**5. Privacy & Security**:
- Is my medical information secure?
- Who will see my medical records?
- Do you comply with privacy regulations?

**6. Qualifications & Credentials**:
- Are your medical specialists certified?
- What qualifications do your doctors have?
- Is your AI system medically validated?

### Q&A Response Best Practices

**Format**:
- Keep answers concise (100-300 words)
- Use bullet points for clarity
- Include relevant links
- Add a call-to-action

**Tone**:
- Professional yet friendly
- Empathetic and reassuring
- Confident but not arrogant
- Patient-focused

**SEO**:
- Include keywords naturally
- Answer completely
- Use location terms (Saudi Arabia, Riyadh, etc.)
- Link to relevant website pages

## Part 5: Messaging Optimization

### Enable Messaging

**Benefits**:
- Direct customer communication
- Faster response than email
- Builds trust and engagement
- Improves local SEO ranking

**Setup**:
1. Enable in GBP dashboard
2. Download Google Business Profile app
3. Set up notifications
4. Assign team member for responses

### Message Response Guidelines

**Response Time**:
- Target: Within 1 hour during business hours
- Maximum: 24 hours
- Enable auto-reply for after-hours

**Auto-Reply Template**:
```
Thank you for contacting Smart Medical Consultant! 🏥

We've received your message and will respond within [X hours/24 hours]. 

For urgent medical matters, please visit our website: [link]

For emergencies, please call 997 (Saudi Arabia emergency services).

- Smart Medical Consultant Team
```

**Common Message Scenarios**:

**1. General Inquiry**:
```
Hi [Name], thank you for your interest in Smart Medical Consultant! [Answer their question]. Would you like to schedule a consultation? Visit [link] or let me know if you have any other questions. - SMC Team
```

**2. Pricing Question**:
```
Hi [Name], great question! Your first consultation is completely FREE and includes AI analysis, specialist review, and comprehensive reports. Subsequent consultations are [price]. Would you like to get started? [link] - SMC Team
```

**3. Service Availability**:
```
Hi [Name], yes, we're available 24/7 for medical consultations! You can submit your medical reports anytime through our website [link], and we'll have your analysis ready within 24-72 hours. How can we help you today? - SMC Team
```

**4. Language Question**:

## Part 6: Insights & Analytics Tracking

### Key Metrics to Monitor Weekly

| Metric | What It Means | Target | Action If Low |
|--------|---------------|--------|---------------|
| **Profile Views** | How many people viewed your profile | Increasing trend | Add more posts, photos |
| **Search Queries** | What people searched to find you | Relevant medical terms | Optimize keywords |
| **Website Clicks** | Clicks to your website | 20%+ of views | Improve photos, description |
| **Direction Requests** | Requests for directions | 10%+ of views (if physical location) | Verify address accuracy |
| **Phone Calls** | Calls from GBP | 5%+ of views | Add clear CTA, verify phone |
| **Photo Views** | Views of your photos | High engagement | Add more photos regularly |

### Monthly Performance Review

**Questions to Ask**:
1. Which posts got the most engagement?
2. What search queries are people using?
3. Are website clicks increasing?
4. Which photos are most viewed?
5. What days/times get most engagement?

**Optimization Actions**:
- Create more content similar to top-performing posts
- Optimize for top search queries
- Post at peak engagement times
- Add more photos in popular categories
- Update underperforming sections
